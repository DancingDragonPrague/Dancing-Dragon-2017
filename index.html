STORY OF DRAGON

How Dancing Dragon was born...

Once upon a time there was a forgotten continent called Atlantis. Mysterious place where the peace and abundance for all beings were guaranteed. At this time, the continent ruled by wise men with the help of priests and scientists. Beings in the universe lived together in peace and together they formed an almost perfect society.

Unfortunately, nothing in our reality is perfect. There is always a balance. At the same time Atlantis was also inhabited by beings which knew only arrogance and power over others. At this time the Dragons were incarnated into human bodies to protect and defend nature and help people to maintain a balance between good and evil. They taught them wisdom and kindness to others, how to work with the ego and the higher self.

These dragons descended on Gaia whenever the balance between positive and negative forces was disrupted. Many prominent figures of history who maintained a balance, been incarnated dragons from the higher dimensions of reality.

Now we are on the threshold of a new age. Age when again and again there is a shift of balance toward the negative forces of this world. Given that this is a completely natural process of Gaia, is once again the need for assistance from dragons to maintain a balance, protect plant and animal kingdom, and help the planet to raise a higher frequency.
